My Interests

  • Swimming is one of my favorite things to do! The typical events I swim is the 100 back and 200 freestyle. I swim on the parks and recreation team, along with my high school varsity team, and am the head captain. On top of swimming, I enjoy teaching children how to swim, and coaching older kids on the parks and rec team. I also work as a lifeguard at my hometown pool.

  • Drawing is really fun as I can let my creativity fly. I prefer drawing with pencil, as I frequently need to erase. I like to draw people, animals, and landscapes, but it depends on the day what I want to draw.

  • Programming, working with many different programming languages. I even programmed this! My favorite language to work with due to versatility, is Java.

  • Painting is a more recent find of mine, and is a big way how I relax when I'm really stressed. It's therapeutic in its own way. I love to paint landscapes but also animals, and find people really hard to paint. I like painting with acrylics, but haven't really tried oil painting yet.

  • Music is a big hobby of mine. I love playing music and listening to music. I play 3 instruments: flute, piccolo, and piano along with singing. I can also kind of play the guitar, uklelele, and percussion instruments. Some of my favorite artists and bands to listen to are AJR, Jax, and Aerosmith. I am a Spotify lover all the way.

  • Spending time with my dogs which includes running around with them, sleeping with them, and petting them. They are so sweet and love us so much. My dogs' names are Toby and Nala; Toby 3 years old and Nala 6 years old. Both can be anxious dogs, but they still love us all the same.
